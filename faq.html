Who is Jake from ENHYPEN?

Jake is an Australian-Korean member of the K-pop boy group ENHYPEN, serving as a vocalist and dancer. He was born Sim Jaeyun on November 15, 2002, in Seoul, South Korea, but grew up in Brisbane, Australia, which gives him a unique bicultural background. Jake is known for his fluent English skills, warm personality, and his signature bright smile that has endeared him to fans worldwide. He successfully auditioned for Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE) and moved to South Korea to train, eventually competing on the survival show I-LAND where he secured his position in ENHYPEN by ranking fourth in the final voting. His ability to bridge Korean and international audiences has made him an invaluable member of the group.

What is Jake ENHYPEN's real name?

Jake's real name is Sim Jaeyun, written as 심재윤 in Korean characters. The surname Sim (심) is a common Korean family name, while Jaeyun (재윤) is his given name. He adopted "Jake" as his English name during his time growing up in Australia, and it became his official stage name when he debuted with ENHYPEN. In Korean contexts, he is often referred to as Jaeyun or Sim Jaeyun, while international fans and English-language media typically use Jake. This dual naming reflects his multicultural identity and helps him connect with both Korean and international audiences. The name Jake has become synonymous with his cheerful personality and has been embraced by fans globally.

When did Jake debut with ENHYPEN?

Jake debuted with ENHYPEN on November 30, 2020, with their first mini-album titled BORDER: DAY ONE. The debut came after he was selected as one of seven final members through the Mnet survival show I-LAND, which aired from June to September 2020. During the show's finale, Jake received 1,179,633 votes from global viewers, securing the fourth position out of 23 initial contestants. The debut showcase was held at the Seoul Olympic Hall, and the title track "Given-Taken" marked ENHYPEN's official entry into the K-pop industry. The album was commercially successful, selling over 450,000 copies and establishing ENHYPEN as one of the most promising fourth-generation K-pop groups. Since debut, Jake has participated in all of ENHYPEN's releases and promotional activities.
